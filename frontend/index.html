Help Center

Clear explanations for key terms and the agentic workflow, written for users who are not finance experts.

What is this dashboard?

This is a digital advisor toolkit that helps you review a client’s financial picture, identify opportunities, and deliver a recommendation package quickly. The system uses AI agents to automate research, compliance checks, and client-ready messaging.

How the workflow works

  • Client Profile Ingestion: gathers the client’s basic details, goals, and risk profile.
  • Outside Asset Detection: finds accounts the client has outside your firm so recommendations are complete.
  • AI Risk Assessment: checks whether the client’s portfolio matches their stated risk tolerance.
  • Tax, Portfolio, Alternative Assets, and Estate agents: run in parallel to recommend tax savings, portfolio balance, new asset opportunities, and legacy planning.
  • Compliance Guardrails: validates the recommendations for suitability and regulatory alignment.
  • Advisor Review: the final human review step before delivery.
  • Client Delivery: prepares the final package for client presentation.
  • Continuous Monitoring: keeps an eye on the client’s portfolio after delivery.

Glossary

AUM
Assets under management. The total dollar value of the client’s investments that you currently oversee.
Unrealized P&L
The paper gain or loss on investments that have not been sold yet.
Outside Assets
Investments or accounts the client has at another institution that are not currently managed here.
Risk Tolerance
How much investment ups and downs the client is comfortable accepting.
Compliance Guardrails
Automated checks that make sure recommendations are appropriate and follow rules.

Why this matters

Most clients do not speak finance jargon. These explanations keep your conversations simple, help you explain the recommendation rationale, and protect the client experience from confusion.
